Clay Sculpting

Clay Sculpting - I attend a clay sculpting class every Wednesday evening. It's a great way to relax and get in touch with my creative side. I enjoy working with my hands and shaping the clay into whatever design I please.

The class is small, so there's plenty of individual attention from the instructor. She's very knowledgeable and always has helpful suggestions on how to improve our sculptures. I've learned a lot and had a great time doing it.
