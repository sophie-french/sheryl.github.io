Hello, my name is

I was born in raised in New York, I grew up in the Bronx and now live in Brooklyn. At college I studied a Fine Arts degree specialising in sculptural installations, and now work for a creative events agency as a junior art director. I also have a podcast calld The Malleable Adult that I named after my love of play-doh. I have a pet rabbit named Sylvan and he is lord of the apartment. I love all things arty and foody, especially if it's pink and sparkly.

I'm pretty into animal crossing right now (I have a switch but I'm playing it on my phone).

I'm passionate about art and creativity, and I love finding new ways to express myself. I'm also really interested in personal growth and development, and love learning new things. I'm always up for a chat, so feel free to send me a message if you want to chat about anything!

My Podcast

The Malleable Adult is a podcast from 24 year-old New York native and child at heart Cheryl. After being told her whole life she needs to grow up and start acting her age, Cheryl started a podcast embracing her youthful side, from her love of play-doh to her hatred of brussel sprouts. Each week Cheryl has on a new guest to discuss ways they're healing and holding onto their inner child, and share tales of how their 'adulting' has gone wrong.
